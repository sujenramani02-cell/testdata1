Here is a quick and charming short story about a clever rabbit who outsmarts a hungry lion by using his own
 reflection in a well.Once upon a time, a fierce lion ruled the forest, terrorizing the other animals by 
hunting them every single day. Exhausted and terrified, the animals decided to hold a meeting. 
They approached the lion and made a deal: every day, one animal would willingly come to him as his meal, 
so that the rest of the forest could live in peace. The lion agreed.One day, it was the turn of the clever 
little rabbit. The rabbit took his time and intentionally arrived very late to the lion's den. The hungry 
and impatient lion roared, "Why are you so late?"
